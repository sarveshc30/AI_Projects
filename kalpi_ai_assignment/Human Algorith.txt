1. Navigate to Kalpi.ai
2. Click on Product Overview -> Strategy Builder

3. Click on Universe Box 
3.1 Select Universe Dropdown
3.2 Choose a Universe

4. Click on filters box
4.1 Click on add condition
4.2 Add operator and number
4.3 repeat for list of conditions

5. Click on Ranking and Scoring
5.1 Select what metrics you want for the ranking
5.2 repeat for items in list

6. Click on weights
6.1 Enter No of Stocks
6.2 Select either equal weight or metric weight
